A Sample Of A Postnatal Record

Postnatal Records for Child: This an assessment done on a new born baby right after birth to 6 weeks after birth. This check-up involves monitoring the growth of the baby, activities done by the baby and to look out for any abnormalities in the baby and to give the appopriate care and treatment to the baby.
