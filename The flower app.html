Some Types of flowers

Let's look at them below

  • Annuals

    • These are flowers that complete their lifecycle within a year. Eg sunflowers

  • Perennials

    • These flowers live and flower for multiple years. Eg coneflowers

  • Biennials

    • These flowers die in their second year of live. Eg foxglove

How to care for flowers if you have any

Let's look into that

  1. Trim the stem

    2. Cut 1-2 inches off at a 45 degrees angle before placing them in water. This helps with water absorbtion.

  2. Use clean vase and fresh water

    3. Always use a clean vase and use room temperature water and change it every 2-3 days.

  3. Avoid sunlight

    4. Avoid direct sunlight, heat, or drafts. Flowers can last longer in cool places.
